Code: UC7.
Name: Yeu cau nhap hang.
Brief Description: Bo phan dat hang quoc te yeu cau cac Site cung cap san pham.
Main flow:
1. Bo phan dat hang quoc te kiem tra so luong hang hoa va ngay van chuyen hang o cac Site: cac Site thoa man.
2. Gui so luong hang hoa mong muon cho cac Site neu cac site thoa man yeu cau dat hang.
Alternative flow:
1. Cac mat hang ko the thoa man o 1 Site.
2. Chia nho don hang, yeu cau don hang tu nhieu Site.
3. Gui yeu cau.
Exception Flow:
1. Cac mat hang ko thoa man o 1 Site.
2. Cac mat hang ko thoa man o tat ca cac Site.
3. Dua ra thong bao loi~
