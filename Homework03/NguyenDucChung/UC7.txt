Code: UC7.
Name: Yeu cau nhap hang.
Brief Description: Bo phan dat hang quoc te yeu cau cac Site cung cap san pham.
Pre-conditions:
- Da dang nhap voi tai khoan bo phan dat hang quoc te
- Bo phan dat hang da chon duoc cac Site nhap hang thoa man
Main flow:
1. Nguoi dung an vao nut gui yeu cau.
2. He thong hien thi form cac mat hang.
3. Nguoi dung dien cac thong tin ve mat hang:
  - Site code
  - Merchandise code
  - Quantity ordered
  - Unit
  - Delivery means.
3. Nguoi dung gui thong tin len he thong.
4. He thong luu lai.
